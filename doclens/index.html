DocLens — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2026

DocLens is built to be private by design. Your documents never leave your phone. This policy explains exactly what the app does and does not do.

What we collect

Nothing. DocLens does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data or document content. There are no accounts and no analytics servers that we operate.

Where your data lives

All scans, images, recognised text (OCR) and settings are stored locally on your device, in the app's private storage. Scan images and the database (including recognised text and tags) are encrypted at rest with AES-256 using a key held in your device's hardware-backed keystore. Deleting a scan or uninstalling the app removes that data from your device.

On-device processing

Document capture, edge detection, perspective correction, image enhancement, OCR (text recognition), translation, redaction, signing and barcode reading are all performed on your device. No images or text are sent to us or any third party for processing.

When the network is used

The app requests internet access only for these optional purposes:

  1. Model downloads: the first time you use a non-Latin OCR language or translation, Google's on-device ML Kit may download the relevant language model to your device. After that it works offline.
  2. Google Play Billing: purchases and subscriptions are processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Permissions

  • Camera — to capture scans. Used only while you are scanning.
  • Internet — for the optional purposes listed above only.
  • Biometric / device credential — only if you enable the optional app lock, to unlock the app. We never store your biometric data (handled by Android).

Storage you control

Exporting, sharing, saving to your gallery or printing happens only when you choose to, to the destination you select. Redaction removes the underlying pixels from the exported copy.

Children

DocLens is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published here with a new "last updated" date.
